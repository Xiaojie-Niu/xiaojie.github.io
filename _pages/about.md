## 关于我 / About Me

你好！我是**牛晓杰**，北京师范大学教育学部教育技术学院的博士候选人，导师是张婧婧教授。我的研究聚焦于**学习科学、学习分析和教育复杂性**，致力于理解在线学习环境中的集体智慧涌现机制。

Hello! I'm **Xiaojie Niu**, a PhD candidate in Educational Technology at Beijing Normal University, supervised by Prof. Jingjing Zhang. My research focuses on **Learning Sciences, Learning Analytics, and Educational Complexity**, with a particular interest in understanding collective intelligence emergence in online learning environments.
